About ReValue One-Click

UK’s AI-powered circular commerce decision engine transforming how products are valued, routed, and reused.

What We Do

ReValue One-Click turns a single photo into an instant, executable resale, trade-in, refurbishment, or recycling decision. Instead of forcing users to search multiple platforms, our AI delivers the best route in one click.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ReValue One-Click and how it works.

What is ReValue One-Click? +
ReValue One-Click is an AI-powered circular commerce platform that helps users instantly decide whether to sell, trade-in, refurbish, or recycle a product using a single image.
How does the AI valuation work? +
Our AI engine analyses product condition, category trends, partner policies, and logistics factors to generate a confidence-based resale or trade-in recommendation.
Is there any cost to use the platform? +
Basic valuation is free. A small execution fee may apply only when a transaction is completed through our routing engine.
How is ReValue different from resale marketplaces? +
Unlike traditional marketplaces, ReValue does not require manual listing or price comparison. It provides a one-click optimised decision across resale, trade-in, and recycling options.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. ReValue follows UK GDPR principles with secure cloud infrastructure, encryption protocols, and privacy-first data architecture.
